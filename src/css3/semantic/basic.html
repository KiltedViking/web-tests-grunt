Buttons

Semantic UI has a wide variety of buttons, made more with the following:

  • Colour: red, orange, yellow, olive, green, teal, blue, violet, purple, pink, brown, grey, and black.
  • Size: mini, tiny, small, medium, large, big, huge, and massive.
  • Variations: positive/negative - green/red, fluid - container width, circle - round.
