Glyphicons

Glyphicons in Foundation (6) is an add-on, i.e. needs linking in of foundation-icons.css. Button examples show that foreground colour adapts to background colour for best contrast.

Buttons

Buttons are buttons (button and submit), but can also be created from anchors (a). Appearence can be varied using:

  • Size: tiny, small, and large, as well as expanded.
  • Colouring: primary, secondary, success, warning, and alert.
  • Other properties: hollow and disabled.
